About Us

Chi-She-Mo

A web application that helps you decide what to eat. It is designed to simplify the process of choosing what to eat by providing a list of food items to choose from. The application is designed to be user-friendly and easy to use. It is also designed to be responsive and work on all devices.

Our team is made up of a group of passionate individuals who are dedicated to making the process of choosing what to eat easier. We are committed to providing a user-friendly and easy to use application that is responsive and works on all devices.
